═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
HOW TO FIX YOUR INCOGNITO ISSUE:
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

**STEP 1: Export from Regular Browser**
1. Open your regular browser tab (where you created the 2 students)
2. Look for the new data management bar at the top
3. Click "📥 Export Data" button
4. A file will download: `students-backup-YYYY-MM-DD.json`

**STEP 2: Import to Incognito Browser**  
1. Open your Incognito tab
2. Click "📤 Import Data" button
3. Select the downloaded JSON file
4. Your 2 students will now appear! 🎉
